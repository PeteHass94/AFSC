Our May Meeting

Our last meeting before the season ends!

Back to the regular Thursday Slot in Blakes of the Hollow for our free to attend Arsenal Supporters Club meeting!

See you at 8pm in the Atrium Bar!

As it's our last one we will be reflecting on our season and of course Arsene Wenger's Reign.

As you can image April has been full of talking points since the last meeting. Wenger's resignation being the pinnacle story of football, we are also in the Semi Finals of the Europa League and no points away from home in 2018 are all part of the roller-coaster experience of being an arsenal fan... what do you make of our month? Have your say on Thursday!
