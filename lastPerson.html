Last Person rules

  • Pick 1 team per game week you think will win.
  • Cannot pick the same team twice!
  • If your team wins you are through to the next game week.
  • If your team loses or draws then you are out.
  • Progress through the gameweeks, the competition keeps going until we have a LAST PERSON STANDING!!

Special Circumstance rules

  • Should all participants of that game week go out
    • The gameweek is void and all participants are through to the next round.
  • Should a fixture get postponed or canceled.
    • Your position is frozen until that fixture is played.
    • Your position is an assumed win and you will have to pick future gameweek teams accordingly.
    • Should your team draw or lose, then your progress is haulted at the gameweek that fixture was suppose to be played.
